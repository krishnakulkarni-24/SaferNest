Awareness & Precautions

Fire Safety

  • Install and test smoke alarms monthly.
  • Keep extinguishers accessible; know the PASS method.
  • Plan two exit routes from every room.

Flood Safety

  • Move to higher ground; avoid walking or driving through flood water.
  • Prepare go-bags with water, food, and medicines.
  • Turn off electricity at the main breaker if instructed.

Earthquake Preparedness

  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances.
  • Practice Drop, Cover, and Hold On.
  • Keep shoes and flashlights near the bed.

Health Emergencies

  • Maintain a first-aid kit and essential medications.
  • Learn CPR and basic first aid.
  • Store emergency contacts in your phone and on paper.

Electrical & Power Safety

  • Avoid overloading outlets; use surge protectors.
  • Unplug devices during storms when safe.
  • Report downed power lines; stay at least 10 meters away.
